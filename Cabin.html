7X Ranch Hunting Cabin

Our hunting cabin is simple and sweet, perfect for 1-2 people looking for a cozy mountain stay. The cabin is equipped with electricity, heat, 1 bed, a kitchenette, and an outhouse. The cabin is located on ranch property, not far from the castle and carriage house. It is great for those looking to hike, explore the BLM, hunt, etc.
