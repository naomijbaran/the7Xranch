The 7X Ranch Castle

The Castle was built in 1905. This old house is full of character and beauty. We took on the project of fully restoring it to it's former glory, and couldn't be happier! It is now equipped with 3.5 bathrooms, 7 bedrooms, full kitchen, 4 fireplaces, and plenty of room for fun and games. The castle is wrapped with both an enclosed porch and an exposed deck. The yard and gardens are the perfect place to host family reunions, weddings, and get-togethers. There is a patio for grilling, an outdoor fire pit for s'mores and late night chats, and not to mention it's prime location. The castle is the centerpoint of the 7X Ranch, home to 500+ cattle, horses, chickens, wildlife of all kinds, streams, and so much more. Since this is a fully functional ranch, you can have the opportunity to meet all our animals! If interested in hunting on our property or the BLM, just feel free to contact us and we can discuss that in detail. The BLM backs onto our property, so hiking, hunting, or riding are always available. Come and experience this truly wonderful place for yourself.

Pricing: $700 per night.
