The 7X Ranch Guest House

Our Guest house is the perfect place to stay if you have a small group of friends or family and want to enjoy God's creation. Sleeps up to 4 people,2 queen size beds. The guest house has also been fully remodeled, equipped with 2 bedrooms, 1 full bath, a fully functional kitchen, and a living room. The guest house boarders the horse pasture, so each morning you can be greeted by our four-legged friends.

Price: $250 per night.
